European Social Survey - Data Analysis

Information on the European Social Survey

The European Social Survey provides a variety of data for comparative analysis that can be downloaded as csv file. Data is collected on various topics such as politics, well-being, socio-demographics, human values etc. This project concentrates on the parsing and plotting of socio-demographic data from the European Social Survey round 10 published in 2020.

The Country Variable

Intending country comparisons the main variable of interest is the country variable. To get a first overview of the data the country variable is counted and displayed in a bar chart showing the number of participants per partaking country.

The Household Income Variable

An interesting data point in the dataset are the different household incomes for the different countries. Those are represented in deciles. The researchers of the ESS aimed for comparable definitions of the respective deciles. Further information can be found in the ESS guidance PDF here.
